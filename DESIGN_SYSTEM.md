# Poolsuite Oasis Design System

## 🎨 Color Palette
- **Sands Cream** (`#FDF6E3`): The main architectural background. Warm, high-end, sun-drenched.
- **Deep Shadow** (`#2E2E2E`): Sharp, high-contrast shadows and primary text.
- **Azure Pool** (`#4C9AD2`): The vibrant, glistening water.
- **Suntan Glow** (`#FF6F61`): Accent color for interactive elements.

## 📐 Layout Principles
- **Brutalist Minimalism**: Sharp edges, large white spaces, and a grid-based layout.
- **The Horizon**: The screen is split between the "Architecture" (top/mid) and the "Pool" (bottom).
- **Negative Space**: Heavy use of open air and floating elements.

## 🌊 Animations
- **Pool Ripples**: Subtle, slow-moving SVG distortions for the water.
- **Sun Shift**: Linear gradients that drift slowly to simulate the passage of a hot afternoon.
- **Drift**: Content fades in with a slow translateY translation.
